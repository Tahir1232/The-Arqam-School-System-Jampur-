Admissions

We welcome new students throughout the academic year. Follow the steps below to join us.

  • Visit our campus at Mohala Jaman Shah, Jampur.
  • Meet our admissions officer and receive an application form.
  • Submit the completed form along with the required documents.
  • Attend an interview and placement test (if applicable).
  • Get admission confirmation and begin the orientation process.
